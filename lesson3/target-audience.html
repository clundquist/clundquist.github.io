Target Audience

The target audience for the website is for all demographics, who are seeking up to the minute weather forecasts. These are individuals who have a desire to know how they should prepare for the day, and week. They are comfortable with using mobile applications and would be able to utilize the site from their mobile device, as well as from their home computer.
