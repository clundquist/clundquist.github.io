Design Principles Document

Chris Lundquist

Repitition

University Place CrossFit

universityplacecrossfit.com

University Place CrossFit uses the principle of repition thru the use of the same color and elements throughout their page. This provides a clear sense of unity, consistency, and cohesiveness.

White Space

Rogue Fitness

roguefitness.com

Rogue Fitness uses the principle of whitespace, by separating all of the page elements equally. Each section on the page uses either vertical or horizontal workspace which provides clear separation and easy navigation around the page.

Contrast

SugarWod

sugarwod.com

SugarWod's site uses the principle of contrast through visual weight. The primary focal point starts with their picture, then moves down to their headline, followed by their purpose. These items capture the interest of the reader leading them to start button to sign-up for their services.
