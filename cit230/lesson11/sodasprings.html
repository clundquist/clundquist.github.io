World's Only Man Made Geyser

by Ned Schnibley

The Soda Springs geyser is billed as the world's only man made geyser. It was created in 1937 when drillers searching for thermal water for a heated medicinal pool went down 315 feet unleashing what became known as the Soda Springs geyser. The feature originally spewed freely until it began to affect the geysers at Yellowstone. It has since been capped and placed on a timer.
