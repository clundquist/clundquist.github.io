Pancakes in the Park!

by Buttery Bros

There’s no better way to spend a Saturday morning than to experience the magic of Maple Sugar season. Start your day by enjoying a hearty breakfast of sausage and pancakes with real maple syrup, along with hot chocolate, juice or milk. Local maple syrup and candies will also be offered for sale. After breakfast don’t miss the self-guided demonstration stations allowing you to get a close-up look at the maple sugaring process throughout the years. The cost of the Pancakes in the Park breakfast is $5.00 per person for those 5 and over. Children 4 and under are free.
